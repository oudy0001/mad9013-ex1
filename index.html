The Moon is Really Big

But not as Big as the Earth

But it's Still Pretty Big

The moon is made of mostly rock with lots of dust which is the result of millions of years of astroids hitting the moon's surface. These astriod collisions pulverized the rock which was once molten and creates the dust which lays almost undisturbed on the moons surface until the next astriod hits.
